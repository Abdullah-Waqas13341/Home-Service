How It Works

Discover how you can easily avail services and grow your business with our platform

About

Our home service application connects customers and service providers seamlessly. Customers can browse and book services like cleaning, electrical work, plumbing, painting, and more. Sellers can easily register their services and manage them through their personalized dashboard. Admins oversee the services, approving or rejecting new services while ensuring quality and reliability.

Registration and Authorization

👤
Customer

Register, book services, and manage your bookings effortlessly.

💼
Seller

Register, add services, and manage them through your personalized dashboard.

👥
Admin

Oversee and manage the platform, approve or reject services.

Users can register with their name, gender, age, email, password, and role (Customer or Seller). Admins are added by default. Login is email-based, and after 5 incorrect login attempts, accounts are locked for 5 minutes.

Customer Dashboard

  • View all available services
  • Sort services by date
  • Search services by category
  • View detailed information on each service
  • Book services
  • Rate and review services after availing them
  • Make payments via Stripe
  • View ongoing and completed services

Admin Dashboard

  • Manage service categories (Add/Delete)
  • Approve or reject newly added services
  • Provide reasons for rejection
  • View lists of approved, rejected, and pending services

Seller Dashboard

  • View all services posted by you
  • Check the status of each service (pending, approved, rejected)
  • Resolve admin comments on rejected services and request re-approval
  • Register new services
  • View reviews submitted for your services
