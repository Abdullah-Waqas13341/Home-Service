Welcome, Seller!

As a seller on our platform, you can showcase your services to a wide range of customers. Here are a few things you need to know before posting a service:

  • Ensure that your service fits into one of the pre-assigned categories.
  • Provide a clear and concise title and description of your service.
  • Set a competitive price for your service.
  • Once submitted, your service will be reviewed by the admin before being published.

Once your service is approved, it will be visible to potential customers. You can also manage your services and update them as needed. If your service is rejected, you'll receive feedback to help you improve it.
